FAQ

The questions every security team asks.

Does Reattend train its model on our data?+
Never. Your memory is single-tenant and isolated. Rabbit's base weights are frozen for your org. Fine-tuning happens in your tenant only, on data you control.
Where is data stored, and who can see it?+
EU or US tenancy of your choice. RBAC-gated at the record level — the LLM never sees memories the asker can't see. Every retrieval is logged and auditable.
What happens to memory when an employee leaves?+
Their personal scratchpad is purged on their offboarding day. Their contributions to org memory — decisions, threads, docs — stay where they belong: with the org.
How does the Chrome extension handle private tabs?+
It doesn't observe them. Period. Personal Gmail, personal Notion, anything outside the workspaces you've explicitly allow-listed is invisible to Reattend.
Can I deploy on-premise or in an air-gapped environment?+
Yes. Government and Enterprise plans include on-premise and air-gapped deployment. We dispatch a trainer onsite if needed.
How long does setup take?+
Most teams are running by end of day. Connect your sources, let the indexer warm up overnight, and your org graph is ready by Friday.
