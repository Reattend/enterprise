How an integration works

Three steps
01 · Connect

OAuth, scoped to read.

Pick an app. Authorize with the narrowest scope that gets the job done. No service accounts, no shared secrets, no inbox passwords.

02 · Filter

Decide what enters memory.

Channels, repos, calendars, lists. Block PII fields, exclude private DMs, redact incident channels. Filters are policy — versioned and auditable.

03 · Index

Quiet, dedup, governed.

We index incrementally, dedupe across sources, and tag every record with provenance. Disconnect anytime — your data leaves with you.
