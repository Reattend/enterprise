A few quick answers

Is there a free trial?
30 days on Team and Pro, no credit card required. Enterprise and Government go through sales so we land you on the right deployment.
What is the difference between Team and Pro?
Pro adds the meeting recorder. A bot joins your Zoom, Meet, or Teams calls, transcribes them, extracts decisions and action items, and saves the result as a memory record (still RBAC-respecting). It replaces Fireflies, Otter, or Granola in your stack. Everything else is identical to Team.
Per-seat. What counts as a seat?
Any active user with an org membership. Guests (read-only on explicit shares) don't count. Admins do.
How much meeting recording is included on Pro?
40 hours per user per month, transcribed and summarised. Audio is retained for 30 days by default; transcripts and summaries stay forever. Heavier usage available on request.
On-prem — what does that actually mean?
The full stack runs inside your network: app, database, and the LLM (via on-prem Rabbit). Zero egress to Reattend. Customer manages updates on their cadence; we provide signed tarball releases.
How fast can we go live?
Team: 30 minutes (Slack + Notion connectors are 1-click). Enterprise dedicated: 1–2 weeks. Government on-prem with paper digitization: 6–12 weeks depending on legacy archive size.
What about the AI engine?
Cloud deployment uses our managed AI stack — frontier reasoning models for answering plus a fast model for triage. Year-2 we ship on-prem Rabbit so the model lives on your hardware. You can swap at any time.
