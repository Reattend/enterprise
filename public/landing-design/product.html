Chapter 01

Capture everything that matters.

Memory only works if it actually exists. Reattend gives your organization a hundred surfaces to drop a thought, a decision, a screenshot, a meeting — without thinking about where it goes. The platform sorts, links, and indexes in the background, so the friction of recording knowledge approaches zero.

  • Slack, Teams, Discord — capture from any channel with a slash command or react-to-save
  • Browser extension pins web pages, Notion docs, Figma frames, and PDFs in one click
  • Meeting capture for Zoom, Meet, and Teams with diarized transcripts and decision extraction
  • Email forwarding to a dedicated org address auto-classifies threads into projects
  • OCR-aware uploads turn screenshots, whiteboards, and scanned docs into searchable text
  • Voice memos transcribed and threaded into the right project automatically

Chapter 02

Connect knowledge into a graph.

Disconnected notes are not memory. Reattend builds a typed graph of every entity in your organization — people, projects, decisions, customers, vendors, contracts — and silently links related entries as they arrive. The result: ask one question, get the whole story back.

  • Entity extraction across messages, docs, and meetings — no manual tagging required
  • Decision chains preserve the why behind every change, with the people who weighed in
  • Blast-radius analysis shows everything a decision affects before you ship the change
  • Org graph mirrors your reporting lines so context stays scoped and auditable
  • Customer & vendor timelines roll up every email, ticket, contract, and call
  • Versioned schemas — entities evolve with your business without breaking old links

Once memory is structured, agents can act on it. Reattend ships a library of pre-built operators — onboarding, exit interviews, deal-room drafting, vendor renewals — that read your org's memory, draft outputs, and route them for human approval. You ship work, not summaries.

  • Pre-built agents for hiring, onboarding, exit, vendor reviews, and deal-room prep
  • Every agent reads org memory; nothing is invented from scratch
  • Drafts route to humans for approval — agents never ship without sign-off
  • Custom agents in YAML or our visual builder, no engineering required
  • Triggered by schedule, by event (new hire), or by /run-now from any surface
  • Full audit log of agent inputs, outputs, and approvals

Chapter 05

Govern memory like a regulated system.

An organization's memory is regulated data. Reattend was built compliance-first: cell-level RBAC, write-once-read-many audit trails, redaction pipelines, and data residency you control. Legal sleeps, security signs, IT ships.

  • Cell-level RBAC — control access down to a paragraph or a numeric field
  • WORM audit trails — every read, write, and approval logged immutably
  • SAML 2.0 + OIDC SSO with SCIM provisioning and just-in-time access
  • GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 Type II — pre-mapped controls, ready for your auditor
  • Data residency in 12 regions, including India, EU, US, UK, and Singapore
  • Customer-managed encryption keys with HSM-backed key escrow
