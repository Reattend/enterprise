Chrome extension troubleshooting

The four issues we see most often. If none of these match, email us a screenshot — we usually reply within a business day.

Floating logo pin not appearing on a page?+
The extension only activates on hostnames in your personal whitelist or your org admin's policy. Open the toolbar icon → Settings to add the domain. If your admin manages the policy, ask them to add it; you'll see the change within a minute of policy push.
Capture flashes = instead of ✓?+
The server detected a near-duplicate within the last three minutes. Wait, or change the content meaningfully (different selection, different page, or different note text). Reattend deduplicates aggressively to keep your memory graph clean — the equals sign means "we already have this."
Extension says token rejected?+
Open /app/settings → API keys, generate a new token, paste it into the extension Options page. If you're on SSO, the token may have expired with the parent session — sign in again to mint a fresh one.
Sidebar won't open?+
Reload the extension from chrome://extensions and try Alt+Shift+A. If that doesn't work, fully sign out of Chrome and back in (this resets the extension's privileged page handle). Last resort: uninstall + reinstall — your settings sync from the cloud.
None of those help?+
Email pb@reattend.ai with a screenshot of the extension popup and the page you were on. Include your Chrome version (chrome://version) and the extension version (toolbar icon → About). We usually reply within a business day.
